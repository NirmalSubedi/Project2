Submit Your Art!

To ensure a smooth process, please follow these submission guidelines:

  • Artwork should be JPG, PNG, or GIF format.
  • Provide a descriptive and unique title for your artwork.
  • Include a brief artist statment or description of your inspiration.
  • By submitting, you agree to our Terms and Conditions.

If you have any questions or encounter issues during the submission process, feel free to contact us.

Please take note of the following specifications:

  • Resolution: Minimum 300dpi
  • Aspect Ratio: Maintain the orgininal aspect ratio of artwork
  • Color Mode: RGB for digital submissions
  • File Size: Maximum 10GB

Ensuring your artwork meets these specifications will help showcase it in the best possible way. If you have any technical questions, fell free to contact our support team.

Before submitting your artwork, please carefully read and understand our Terms and Conditions:

  1. By submitting your artwork, you agree to grant us a non-exclusive license to display, reproduce, and distribute the submitted content.
  2. We reserve the right to decline submissions that do not meet our guidelines or standards.
  3. Artists retain ownership of their work, but we may use submitted content for promotional purposes with proper attribution.
  4. Violation of these result in the removal of your artwork from our platform.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Terms and Conditions, feel free to reach out to our team.
